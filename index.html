KOTOKA03 / Insight
Genesis AI 2026

The Insight

Repetition cannot fix what context broke.

Duolingo, Babbel, ELSA optimize repetition. Repetition strengthens encoding only when retrieval context matches encoding context (Godden & Baddeley, 1975). Classroom-encoded vocab fails at the office. Kotoka binds every word to the user's own scene, location, mood, sound, time-of-day, weather — and re-fires the review the moment they walk back in.
